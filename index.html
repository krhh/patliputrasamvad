The Idea

From Stone
to Dialogue

पाषाण से संवाद तक

Pataliputra was not merely a city. For centuries, it was the world's nerve centre for governance, ideas, and knowledge — the birthplace of two great religions, home to Chanakya, capital of the Mauryan empire that once commanded 32% of the global economy.

That spirit of dialogue did not die. It is waiting to be revived.

"Patliputra once shaped the intellectual destiny of a civilization. Perhaps, its conversation can do so again. The dialogue begins."

Patliputra Samvad is that revival. A platform where Bihar's roots become the foundation for its future — where understanding your soil is the first act of building enterprise.

I
The Pataliputra Capital
The flame-palmette carved in Mauryan stone — an ancient symbol of life, rising knowledge, and energy from deep roots. Our logo begins here.
II
The Solar Crown
The spreading form echoes Chhath Puja — Bihar's most sacred tradition and its reverence for the sun as the eternal source of life and renewal.
III
The Infinity of Samvad
At the base, an infinity symbol. Conversation is not an event — it is a continuous process. Eternal knowledge. Unbroken civilizational flow.
IV
Past → Present → Future
The second edition moves from dialogue to participation. Bihar's youth are no longer the audience — they are the architects.

Quick Answers

Common questions

Who can participate?
College and university students from anywhere can join Track I. School students of Class 7–12 can participate in Track II. Teams should ideally have a connection to Bihar, but we welcome students passionate about its development.
Is there a participation fee?
Participation in both tracks is completely free. Selected finalists will travel to Patna for the final conclave in August 2025, where prizes, certificates, and recognition await.
When do registrations open?
Registrations for both tracks open in the first week of May 2025. Register your interest now and you will be the first to know when they go live — including access to the pre-registration workshop series.
Do we need to be from a specific institution?
No. Any student from any college or school can apply directly — no institutional affiliation required. That said, if your institution wants to become a formal Institute Partner, we welcome that too.
